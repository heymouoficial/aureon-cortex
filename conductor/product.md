# Product Guide: Multiversa Lab

## Initial Concept
Multiversa Lab serves as the central platform for designing, organizing, and operating an ecosystem of AI-driven services, including Multiversa, CalculaTú, and HeyMode. It leverages AI agents and automations to generate leads and execute tasks.

## Primary Purpose
The primary purpose of Multiversa Lab is to provide a comprehensive suite of AI-driven services and client applications that integrate into a broader ecosystem, offering solutions across various domains.

## Target Users
The target users for the products developed within Multiversa Lab are diverse, encompassing:
*   **Internal Development Teams:** For the management and orchestration of AI projects and services.
*   **Agencies, Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs), and Entrepreneurs:** Businesses seeking to leverage AI for lead generation, operational efficiency, and enhanced customer engagement through solutions like Multiversa.
*   **End-Consumers:**
    *   **CalculaTú Users:** Buyers looking for financial calculation tools and related services.
    *   **HeyMode Users:** Individuals interested in well-being, mindfulness, and consciousness-enhancing applications.
